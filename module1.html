METHODOLOGY

The 5 Discovery Skills

Innovation is not magic. It is a set of behavioral habits.

  • 1. Questioning: Ask "Why?" like a child. Challenge constraints.
  • 2. Observing: Look for gaps in the real world (not screens).
  • 3. Experimenting: Fail fast to learn fast. (e.g., Ola Labs).
  • 4. Networking: Diverse ideas, not just contacts.
  • 5. Associating: Connecting the dots (Cognitive Skill).
