7 Habits of Creatives

  • 1. T-Shaped Skills: Depth in one, Breadth in many.
  • 2. Hobbies: Art/Music strengthen brain links.
  • 3. Think with Hands: Sketch, build, prototype.
  • 4. Experimental Mindset: Failure = Learning.
  • 5. Half Chances: Spot early opportunities.
  • 6. Discipline: Choose hard over easy.
  • 7. Humor: Don't take yourself too seriously.
