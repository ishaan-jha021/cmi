Challenging Assumptions

Innovation = Attacking what we take for granted.

CASE STUDY: UPI (Unified Payments Interface)

OLD Assumptions

  • Need Account Number.
  • Need IFSC Code.
  • Banking hours matter.
  • Add Beneficiary (wait 30 mins).

NEW System

  • Phone Number is enough.
  • Instant Transfer.
  • 24x7 Access.
  • QR Code Scan.

TOOL #3

4 Tiers of Users

Insight: Biggest growth comes from "Unexplored" users, not loyal ones.
  • Loyal: Smallest group.
  • Dissatisfied: Use out of necessity.
  • Refusing: Consciously avoid you.
  • Unexplored: New markets (Biggest).

Case: DigiYatra (Simplicity for Refusing users)
